about me

what gets me up in the morning?

Hi, I'm Tanya.

I've spent the last 8+ years building digital experiences across a number of consumer technology brands. I'm passionate about the intersection of people, technology, and design, and how we can share making knowledge and skills to empower others to build an inclusive future.

Currently, I'm a third year, Human Centered Design & Engineering student at University of Washington while working full-time at Facebook.

What I love about my job

  • Focus on the person and the community
  • Systems Designs thinking- what should we build and why?
  • Collaborating across teams
  • Building digital experiences- fb.gg/home, facebook.com/creators, and more to come
  • Helping people grow their careers

What do I want?

  • Advance my knowledge of maker tools and skills
  • Build functional and beautiful objects that become an experience
  • Learn new skills that I wouldn't have an opportunity to learn otherwise
  • Be a part of a curious and inclusive community
