Context

I started to think about this project when we did molding and casting. Since I wanted to do something food themed, I thought doing something that tied with that project would be a fun way to do something out of the box. I ended up doing chocolate ice cream cones. From there, I wanted to tie in motion, 3D printing, and laser cutting, which led me to thinking of ferris wheels.

03

Use Case

This could be used at a themed party or event as a fun way to serve ice cream. This would be another way to get more use out of my ice cream cone mold.

breakdown of tasks

CAD modelling

Design the carousel including main body, carts, and moving part.

3D printing

Handle of the moving part of the carousel

Laser cutting

I will be laser cutting as many pieces as possible due to its quicker time to fabricate and ability to use cheaper materials like cardboard as a test before using more expensive materials like wood. This will most likely include the stand, the main rotating body that is holding all of the carts, and the carts.

Mechanism design

There are a few parts that will need to move.

  • Each cart will need to move along a hinge that is connected to the main carousel so the cart will always hang downwards rather than have it be static
  • If I have extra time, I will also make a handle that is connected to the main carousel to make it rotate

Casting

I will use the mold from assignment 6 to cast more chocolate ice cream cones

Finishing

Smooth or paint components so they look more professional
