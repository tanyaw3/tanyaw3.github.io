I'm very grateful for the instructions that Joshua left. It really helped. 04

Export to Bantam Tools

When I exported from KiCad to Bantam Tools I had to change my plan completely. I decided to scrap my original dice idea since the holes in the dice weren't porting over properly. I was running out of time but still wanted to complete the project.



  • Approach 1: Try to still create some box, just without the dice design. However, I couldn't get the walls to fit. So I decided to do a different approach using a slot design.


  • Approach 2: Leverage 2 of the original sides cut and create a slot system that will hold the 2 in place


Once I moved forward with the slot design, I kept milling it until it fit.



kit design and execution

01

CNC Milling

I still have a lot to learn and overcome with CNC milling. Some tips to remember

  • Bring more double sided sticky tape than you think you'll need
  • Triple check Bantam Tools and make sure Thickness and Palcement z-axis are right
  • Make sure Traces is unclicked


02

Assembly Execution Details

Assembly was a lot of trial and error before the final assembly, so by the time I actually had to put things together, it was pretty straight forward.

Once I figured out the tolerances, it was pretty smooth sailing.
